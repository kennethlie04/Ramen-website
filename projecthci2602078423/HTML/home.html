JAPAN’S NATIONAL TREASURE

Ramen is made of hand-pulled wheat noodles and is a traditional Japanese noodle soup. It usually consists of pork or chicken stock and has toppings like scallions, bamboo shoot, etc. It consists of a fish, meat, or vegetable-based broth and tare which is a sauce, that is made of Shoyu or miso.
